Tigers in The Glacial Period VS Tigers Now

These visualisations were made using Kendo UI and data from various sources. They aim to inform the public on various aspects of animal welfare during the holocene. The first visualisation shows the relative population since the year 10000 where 1 equals 10,000,000,000 people compared to the extinction rate per year since that time. The second visualisation shows the weight and the lifespan of over a 1000 different mammals. The last visualisation shows a map of the presence of tigers in various countries during the glacial period and 2020. All sources are cited and all used data sets can be found in the Github repository provided together with this website.
