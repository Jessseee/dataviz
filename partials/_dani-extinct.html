Animals in captivity: Extinct in the Wild

This overview shows which animals are extinct in the wild, only living on in captivity. They are categorized per animal class and continent. Europe is excluded as there are no animals originally from Europe that are extinct in the wild. Data was retrieved from Wikipedia, The IUCN Red List of Threatened Species, and The WWF Wildfinder Database.
